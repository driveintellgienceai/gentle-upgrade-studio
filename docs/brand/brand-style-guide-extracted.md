Brand Typography
We use two typefaces in our brand communication: Mostra Nuova and
Museo Sans.
Mostra Nuova is used primarily for headlines and titles, while Museo Sans is used for body copy and
other text. Both typefaces should be used consistently across all communications.

--- PAGE 8 ---
Brand Applications
Across all mediums, the brand should express modern
simplicity with warmth.
In print, lean on clean white space, thin architectural lines,
and muted earth tones inspired by mid-century design.
Digital assets should mirror the cozy, inviting tone of the
office—use natural textures, subtle shadows, and minimal
gradients.
Merchandise like mugs, apparel, and stationery should
highlight the geometric tooth mark and soft palette,
maintaining the balance between artful and professional.
