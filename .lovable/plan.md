

## Tooth Architect Dental Care — Modernized Website Rebuild

### Overview
Rebuild the full Tooth Architect Dental Care website with the **same gold/dark color scheme**, exact text content, and identical URL structure — but with a cleaner, less cluttered layout, better spacing, and modern component design. Forms will be visual-only for now.

### URL Structure (preserved exactly)
- `/` — Home
- `/meet-the-doctor/` — About Dr. Talebi
- `/contact-us/` — Contact page with form & hours
- `/request-an-appointment/` — Appointment request form
- `/testimonials/` — Patient reviews
- `/gallery/` — Smile gallery
- `/second-options/` — Second Opinion page
- `/botox/` — Botox Injectables
- `/tmj/` — TMJ Treatment
- `/teeth-whitening/` — Teeth Whitening
- `/clear-aligners-reveal-suresmile/` — Clear Aligners
- `/enameloplasty/` — Enameloplasty
- `/privacy-policy/` — Privacy Policy
- `/cookie-policy/` — Cookie Policy
- Blog posts (each as its own page, e.g. `/gum-swelling-treatment-sandy-springs-ga/`)

### Design Modernization
- **Keep**: Gold (#D4A843-ish) + dark navy/charcoal color palette, diamond logo branding
- **Improve**: Much more whitespace, cleaner typography hierarchy, less visual clutter
- **Navigation**: Simplified sticky header with logo, clean nav links, prominent "Request Appointment" CTA button
- **Mobile**: Fully responsive with hamburger menu
- **Sections**: Better visual separation, consistent card-based layouts for services
